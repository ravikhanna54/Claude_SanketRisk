💾 Data Backup & Export

Export all platform data to CSV files for safekeeping. Download and save to your OneDrive or Google Drive. Recommended monthly or after major inspection batches.

🛡️
What gets backed up
This tool exports all database tables: inspection records, inspection requests, user profiles, recommendations, and platform settings — each as a separate CSV file, plus a combined ZIP archive.

Photos are not included in this export as they are stored separately in Supabase Storage. Your original photos remain on the devices used to upload them (phones, tablets, computers). The photo storage paths are recorded in the inspections export so nothing is lost.
